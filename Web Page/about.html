About Image Dehazer

Image Dehazer is an advanced deep learning-based tool designed to enhance the visibility of hazy images and videos. By leveraging a residual-based CNN algorithm, it effectively removes haze and improves image clarity.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide cutting-edge dehazing solutions for photographers, researchers, and everyday users to restore image quality.

Technology Used

  • Residual-based CNN Algorithm
  • NYU-Depth V2 Dataset
  • OpenCV for Image Processing
  • Web Integration for User-Friendly Interface
